## 📌 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing **banking and financial risk data** from American Express.  
It provides insights into **loans, deposits, customer segmentation, and financial behavior**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze **loan distribution and risk exposure**
- Study **deposit patterns across customers**
- Understand **customer segmentation (income, nationality)**
- Identify **high-value clients and engagement trends**
- Evaluate **bank performance metrics**

## 📈 Key Insights

### 💰 Loan Analysis
- Majority of loans come from **high-income customers**
- **Private banking clients** contribute highest loan value
- Long-term loans (>20 years) dominate exposure

---

### 🏦 Deposit Analysis
- Total deposits are **close to $1T**
- **Bank deposits** contribute majority share
- Savings & checking accounts show strong balance distribution

---

### 🌍 Customer Segmentation
- European customers contribute highest deposits
- Followed by Asian and American segments
- Income band analysis shows **mid-income dominates volume**

---

### 📊 Risk & Engagement
- High engagement customers show **higher deposit and loan activity**
- Longer relationship customers bring **higher value**

---

### 💳 Financial Behavior
- Credit card usage is relatively low compared to loans
- Fee contribution is significant (~$42bn)
- Strong dependency on traditional banking services
